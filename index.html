About Us

Mission Statement

To lead the food festival scene by offering global food trends in a way that respects the environment as we reduce, reuse and recycle in every event. To support the community by hiring local employees and talents with a focus on equal opportunity. To make every IFFI event support the effort to bring clean drinking water to remote areas in the Philippines.

Purpose

To offer a new culinary experience to the market as they gather communities and let them unwind through superb food and music.

Vision

To be the preferred food festival organizer offering tasteful delicacies from different places and great, relaxing music that will be enjoyed by the community.

Core Values

IFFI believes in Customer Orientation and will always prioritize the customer’s needs and wants. The company believes in Teamwork knowing that the best results are accomplished when things are done together. IFFI also upholds Integrity & Excellence believing that honesty and giving their best to the clients will win their trust and make IFFI their preferred organizer.

Goals

To become the brand name to reckon with when it comes to food festival and entertainment through our marketing efforts and excellent service. To make every IFFI location a hub for food, with people who passionately prepare food and for music enthusiasts, catering to those who love to eat their hearts out while enjoying and relaxing to enlivening and soothing music.
